This program shows who has the right to receive ration or pension?
- If this person is a man...and his name is Atris, Ahmed, or Muhammad, and a man, and his age is greater than or equal to 30 years. And his marital status is married. He obtains the pension by typing the sentence Go and get the pension.

- And if a girl named Atrisa, Hind, Sarah, or Laila, who is a woman and is over 25 years old and not married, gets the pension.

- And if she has one of the four names shown above. She is over 30 and married and receives a pension.

- وإذا كانت لديها اسم من الاسماء الأربعة الموضحة أعلاه. وسنها أكبر من الثلاثين ومتزوجة تحصل على المعاش.
